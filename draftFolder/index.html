Appetizers

Calla Lily Seafood Soup

A balanced combination of seafood. The delicacy of crab and mint make for a clear soup, and the calla lily brings it a refreshing taste.

Sashimi Platter

A seafood dish made using fresh ingredients. The fish and shellfish are cut into pieces and arranged neatly.

Jade Parcels

An exquisite-looking dish. The ham's sweetness is locked inside the fresh vegetables, drizzled with a spicy broth. Delicious is an understatement.

Panipuri

An appetizer served with a green dipping sauce. The crispy balls are first deep-fried, then stuffed with a delicious filling through a hole on the top.

Consomme

A soup with a recipe involving many steps. Mash ingredients, then stew the mash in broth, and finally serve only the clear soup from the stew.

Main Dish

Barbatos Ratatouille

A simple chowder with a long history. The ingredients are similarly traditional and simple.

Adeptus' Temptation

A complex, famous type of Liyue cuisine, in which specially selected ingredients are submerged and slowly bowled into soup stock.

Invigorating Kitty Meal

This main dish looks rather cute. With Kiminami Anna's help, this dish was made with the tastes of the provisional head priestess Neko in mind.

Tandoori Roast Chicken

A signature chicken dish of Sumeru. The chicken was first marinated with red spices, and then roasted in a tandoor.

Barbeque Ribs

A crispy meat dish. Lightly fry the surface of the ribs until they exude a faint aroma, then wrap the crispy exterior with sauce, and finally, grill them slowly on low heat.

Desserts

Fine Tea, Full Moon

A tea pastry containing egg yolk. The salted yolk is first steamed, then stuffed and wrapped in tea powder-infused dough.

Sakura Mochi

A delicate and elegant snack. The dark green leaf adds complexity to the overall warm color palette.

Rainbow Macarons

Small, multi-colored round pastries. There's a saying in Fontaine that a dessert spread without macarons is like having the many waters bereft of their source

Tricolor Dango

A soft, glutinous snack. Glutinous rice has been grown into powder and rolled into a ball before being steamed.

Padisarah Pudding

Juices are squeezed from the petals of Padisarahs and Sumeru Roses before being mixed with milk, gelatin, sugar, and other ingredients, and then left to solidify.

Beverage

Berry & Mint Burst

A freshly squeezed, fashionable, and fruity non-alcoholic beverage. A refreshing burst of Mint with Berries to sweeten the deal, it has an exquisite aroma.

Fruits of the Festival

A brightly colored non-alcoholic beverage. Freshly squeezed Sunsettia and Wolfhook juices have been poured into a cup in a specific order.

Sparkling Berry Juice

An all-new non-alcoholic beverage. Valberries of varying ripeness add layers of sour-to-sweet to this juice while the cooling sparkling water refreshes the soul
